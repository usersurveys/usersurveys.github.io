User Consent

Hello! Thank you for taking the time to take this survey. Your input is much appreciated.

We're trying to gain an insight on how people decide a piece of information they read online is factually correct or not.

The survey would take about 10 minutes to complete. We also wish to collect some demographical data such as your age, gender. We also wish to collect more private information such as your social media handles which might be used in the future for data collection purposes. This data would only be shared with the researchers working on the project, and complete anonymity of the participants would be maintained. Participation in the study is completely voluntary, and you can back out at any point in time. This is for research purposes only.

Also, the study does not aim to actually gauge whether a news article is true or false, but rather what the user feels about its factual correctness. So the participants are requested to not use the internet to fact check the articles from the internet.

Instructions for Questions

The survey has a total of 15 questions and in each question, you would be provided with a choice of two fictional excerpts (and not picked up from online sources), and you would be asked to choose the excerpt which you are more likely to share and also why you chose a particular excerpt.

You would be provided with three sets of 5 questions, and after completing each set of 5 questions, you would either be able to submit the form and finish the survey or continue with the survey and fill up the next set of 5 questions.
